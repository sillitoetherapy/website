Welcome to Sillitoe Therapy

It can be challenging finding the right therapist for you. I applaud you for being immersed in sometimes the most difficult part of therapy- deciding to meet with someone. Whether it is individual challenges or relationship issues, I am here to support you in achieving your goals for a better life. You are worthy of freedom, love, and happiness. I offer a safe, non-judmental environment in which to explore solutions to sometimes paralyzing obstacles.

My Clients

I enjoy meeting with individuals, couples, and families of all backgrounds. I am particularly well suited for working with the LDS population. I specialize in treating pornography addiction, but also treat other problems such as anxiety, depression, marital conflict, and many others.
